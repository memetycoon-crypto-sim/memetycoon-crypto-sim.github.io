# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26.04.2026.

Moonshot Tycoon: Crypto Sim ("Meme Tycoon") is a parody game about running a fictional meme coin. It is not a wallet, exchange, investment product, or trading platform. No real money, tokens, or trading happen in the app.

## Information You Provide

We do not ask for or collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, address, photos, messages, payment details, wallet addresses, private keys, or financial account information.

The app lets you enter fictional in-game names, coin names, or posts as part of gameplay. This content is used for the game experience and is not intentionally uploaded to our servers.

## Local Game Data

Your game progress and settings are saved locally on your device using Android DataStore. This includes things like your coin name, balance, upgrades, onboarding progress, audio settings, crash-reporting preference, and gameplay counters. This save data stays on your device unless Android's normal app backup system restores it for you.

You can delete local game data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

## Notifications

The app requests `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` on Android 13 and newer so it can send optional gameplay reminders, such as offline earnings or timeline nudges. Notification permission can be denied during the prompt or turned off later in Android system settings.

## Ads and Consent

The app uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. AdMob may process data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, coarse location signals, IP address, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other technical information to serve, limit, measure, and improve ads, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

In regions where consent is required, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage consent before requesting ads. If Google determines that privacy options are required for your region, the app shows a Privacy choices entry in Settings so you can review or change your choices.

## Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics for limited gameplay analytics, such as app opens, onboarding progress, first upgrade purchase, first post submission, rewarded ad completion, and first prestige. We do not intentionally send your name, email address, contacts, payment information, wallet information, or financial account information in analytics events.

Firebase Analytics collection is disabled by default at startup and is enabled only after the consent flow completes and Google indicates that ad requests may be made for this device and region.

## Crash Reports

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to help find and fix crashes. Crash reports can include technical information such as device model, OS version, app version, stack traces, crash timestamps, and limited in-game diagnostic keys like fictional coin name, upgrade count, and current progression values.

Crash reporting is off by default. You can turn it on or off in the app's Settings screen. We do not call Firebase Crashlytics `setUserId`, and we do not intentionally add personal information to crash reports.

## Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your data.

We do not share your data with advertisers, brokers, or unrelated third parties for their own independent use. Google services such as AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google Play, and Android backup may process limited data needed to provide ads, analytics, crash reporting, app distribution, backup/restore, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

## Security

Local game data is stored in the app's private storage area. Google services use encrypted connections in transit.

## Data Deletion

You can clear local app data from Android system settings or uninstall the app to remove local saves from the device. For questions or deletion requests involving Google-processed app data, contact us at leon.alpeza@gmail.com.

## Contact

Questions or deletion requests can be sent to leon.alpeza@gmail.com.
